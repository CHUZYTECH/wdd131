Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: ChuzyTech

Description: ChuzyTech is dedicated to providing expert solutions to various technology-related problems, including troubleshooting, tutorials, and product recommendations.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a central hub for technology enthusiasts, professionals, and beginners to access reliable tech support, guides, and industry updates.

Target Audience

The primary audience includes tech enthusiasts, IT professionals, students, and individuals seeking solutions to tech-related issues.

Scenarios

  • How do I troubleshoot common computer errors?
  • What are the best tools for website development?
  • How can I optimize my PC for better performance?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #10202F (Used for headers and buttons)

Secondary Color: #A1D6CB (Used for backgrounds and accents)

Accent Color: #FFD700 (Used for highlights and call-to-action elements)

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Body Font: Roboto

Accent Font: Lato (Used for quotes and special highlights)
